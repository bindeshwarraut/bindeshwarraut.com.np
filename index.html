Our Tribe

We are a close-knit group of long-standing friends. With backgrounds in design, engineering, product innovation, and creative thinking, we are united by our profound respect for our planet. No pretenses, no B.S. Just a genuine desire to dedicate our lives to a cause we truly believe in. Most of us spent over a decade helping world renowned fashion brands, lead the way in design and innovation. But now, we're stepping away form the corporate wold. Why? We think too much politic and little action results in little processes. We want to make a difference bu creating a brand that is one of its king that attributes important factors like hyper-local production from start to finish, ultra-sustainable by using all things that are bipolar gradable, and ethically made by only prosecuting in the USA. No shortcut, no but, if and why just simply do the right thing.
